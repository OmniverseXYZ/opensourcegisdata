Parker Dinkins February 1, 2023 US Federal

DHS REST Services

Rest Services are a fancy GIS way of saying online datasource of database. The REST Service serves the data over the internet. These sources can be loaded into QGIS and ArcGIS applications by establishing a REST service connction.
